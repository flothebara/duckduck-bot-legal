Duckduck Bot

Privacy Policy

Effective: 22 July 2026

1. Data controller

Duckduck Bot is operated privately by Flo Bara.

  • Discord: soeinflooo
  • Email: baracommunications@gmail.com
  • Support server: Bara Bots

2. Data we process and why

The bot processes Discord user, server, channel, role, message, interaction, and application IDs where they are required to execute commands, enforce permissions, prevent duplicate mutations, operate suggestions, and diagnose administrative changes.

For daily rewards it stores a server-scoped balance, claim time, and transaction history. For suggestions it stores the author, title, description, optional Discord attachment URL, votes, status, decision reason, and related channel and message IDs. Administrative configuration and audit records identify the acting user.

Processing needed to respond to a requested command is based on providing that service. Security, permission enforcement, abuse prevention, auditing, and reliable operation are based on the controller's legitimate interest in operating a safe and dependable private bot. The bot does not make legal or similarly significant automated decisions.

3. Retention

  • Daily claim timestamps: 48 hours.
  • Economy transaction history and administrative audit records: 90 days.
  • Duplicate-prevention receipts: 30 days.
  • Failed suggestion submissions: 7 days.
  • Accepted or denied suggestions: 365 days. Votes are removed when a decision is made.
  • Runtime logs: 14 days.
  • Server data: deleted 14 days after the bot leaves that server unless it rejoins first.
  • Database backups: 30 days.

Current balances, active configuration, and unresolved suggestions are kept while needed to provide the bot. Backup copies are used only for recovery and expire within 30 days. Erased personal data must not be reintroduced during a restore.

4. Sharing and external services

Data is not sold. Discord supplies the platform and hosts messages and attachments. Hosting and database providers process operational data on the controller's behalf. The optional duck-image command requests public photo information from Pexels; it does not send a user's Discord identity to Pexels. These providers may process data outside the European Economic Area under the safeguards described in their own privacy terms.

5. Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or object to processing where the applicable legal requirements are met. You may also complain to a competent data protection supervisory authority. Contact the controller using the details above. The account must be verified before data is exported or erased. Live data is erased after verification; backup copies expire within the stated backup period.

6. Security and changes

Access is limited to the bot operator and protected server processes. Secrets are not stored in this website or repository. Material policy changes will be published on this page with a new effective date.
